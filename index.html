ABOUT

Designer, sharp eye for detail

I'm ShahAzeem — a UI/UX designer who treats every project like the first real shot to prove that good design solves problems, not just decorates screens. I learn fast, ask the right questions, and obsess over the small details most people skip.

My foundation is in user-centered design principles, wireframing, and prototyping — built through coursework, personal projects, and a genuine love for clean, functional interfaces. I'm ready to bring that energy to a real team and a real product.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm studying interfaces I admire, breaking them down to understand why they work.
